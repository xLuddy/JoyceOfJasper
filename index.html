FAQ

  • Do you have any gift references?

    We are grateful for the time and effort you'll spend just to be there on our special day. We'll be thankful to receive monetary gifts as we build our family.

  • What should we wear?

    If you are not part of the entourage, semi-formal attire is required.
    For men: Long sleeves polo and black pants.
    For women: Preferably long or mid-dress.

  • Can we bring our kids to the wedding?

    As much as we love your kids, we want the event only for adults. The only kids attending are those who are part of the entourage. We appreciate your understanding.

  • Why is RSVP so important?

    We need to identify the guests who are attending or not. Your RVSP also helps us to determine the final head count for food serving and seating arrangement.

  • Can I bring a date/plus-one with me?

    Unfortunately, due to the limited capacity of the venue and the number of guests attending, we kindly request that you ONLY bring a guest if you were specifically given a plus one on your invitation. We hope you understand.

  • Where is the reception?

    Lola Berta's Garden and Events Place

  • Can we sit anywhere at the reception?

    We have arranged our seating for everyone's convenience, and you will be seated alongside your family and friends.

  • How can we help the couple have the best time during their wedding?

    Pray with us for favors and provisions.
    Confirm your attendance now.
    Wear appropriate attire.
    Be there on time.
    Stay until the end of the program.
    Participate, enjoy, and have fun.

  • Where is the wedding?

    San Roque Parish Church Pateros
